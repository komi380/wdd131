MILADOU — Basketball Development Program

Site plan: overview, training sessions, youth development, and community engagement.

1. Site Name

MILADOU — name of the basketball development program. (Chosen for its local recognition and simplicity.)

(A simple logo — initial “M” — will be used in the header and favicon.)

2. Site Purpose

The site aims to inform the community about the MILADOU program, present training offers, display training schedules and events, and enable registration and contact.

  • Inform: mission, objectives, and calendar
  • Convert: registration form for participants
  • Promote: gallery, testimonials, and results

3. User Scenarios

  1. Parent — "How do I register my child for the MILADOU program and what are the costs?"
  2. Young player — "What training sessions are available for my level?"
  3. Volunteer/Coach — "How can I help or apply as a coach?"

4. Color Schema

--primary
Usage: titles, links, important accents
--accent
Usage: buttons, calls to action
--bg
Usage: page background
--text
Usage: main text

5. Typography

Chosen fonts:

  • Montserrat — Titles / Logo (Montserrat Bold)
  • Roboto — Body text (comfortable reading on mobile)

7. Pages (minimum 3)

  • Home — mission, CTA, highlights
  • Programs & Schedule — training details and registration form
  • About / Coaches — biographies, curriculum
  • Gallery & News — photos, videos, articles
  • References — sources cited in the footer

8. JavaScript Features

  • Form handling with validation and localStorage
  • Schedule filter (array methods + template literals)
  • Gallery lightbox (DOM + events)
  • Testimonials displayed dynamically
